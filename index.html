Before You Start - Daily Checks

Check These Every Time:

  • Brakes work properly
  • Steering moves smoothly
  • Forks are not bent or cracked
  • Warning lights and horn work
  • No oil or water leaks
  • Tyres are in good condition

üö¶ Click to reveal: The Golden Rules of Forklift Safety

  1. Speed: Never go faster than walking pace
  2. Loads: Keep forks low when moving
  3. Corners: Slow down and sound your horn
  4. People: Never let anyone ride on the forklift
  5. Parking: Lower forks, apply handbrake, remove key

Working Around Forklifts

üë• If You're NOT the Driver

  • Stay away from operating forklifts
  • Never walk under raised loads
  • Make eye contact with drivers before crossing their path
  • Use designated walkways

üö® Real Example

A worker fell 8 metres from forklift forks when trying to change a light bulb. The company was fined ¬£200,000. Never stand on forklift forks or pallets for any reason!

üÜò What to do in an emergency?

  • Stop the forklift immediately
  • Apply the handbrake
  • Turn off the engine
  • Get help from a supervisor
  • Report the incident

üì¶ Pallet Trucks - Manual and Electric

‚ñº

üí° Quick Fact

Pallet trucks cause many back injuries. Most happen because people don't follow simple safety rules.

Manual Pallet Trucks

‚úÖ Safe Use Steps

  1. Check: Wheels roll freely, handle works
  2. Position: Forks fully under the pallet
  3. Lift: Pump handle until load clears floor
  4. Move: Keep load low, push don't pull
  5. Watch: Look where you're going

‚öñÔ∏è Weight Limits Matter

  • Manual trucks: Usually 2-3 tonnes maximum
  • Check the label on your truck
  • Never exceed the limit
  • Get help with heavy loads

Electric Pallet Trucks

Before Each Use:

  • Battery charge level is good
  • Emergency stop button works
  • Forks move up and down smoothly
  • Steering handle is secure
  • Warning devices work
